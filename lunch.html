Cheese beef burger

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
Read more
  • 4 burger buns
  • All or any of the following to serve: sliced tomato, beetroot, horseradish sauce, mayonnaise, ketchup, handful iceberg lettuce, rocket, watercress

Method

  1. 1. Tip 500g beef mince into a bowl with 1 small diced onion and 1 egg, then mix.
Read more
  1. 2. Divide the mixture into four. Lightly wet your hands. Carefully roll the mixture into balls, each about the size of a tennis ball.
  2. 3. Set in the palm of your hand and gently squeeze down to flatten into patties about 3cm thick. Make sure all the burgers are the same thickness so that they will cook evenly.
  3. 4. Put on a plate, cover with cling film and leave in the fridge to firm up for at least 30 mins.
  4. 5. Heat the barbecue to medium hot. Lightly brush one side of each burger with vegetable oil.
  5. 6. Place the burgers, oil-side down, on the barbecue. Cook for 5 mins until the meat is lightly charred. Dont move them around or they may stick.
  6. 7. Oil the other side, then turn over using tongs. Dont press down on the meat, as that will squeeze out the juices.
  7. 8. Cook for 5 mins more for medium. If you like your burgers pink in the middle, cook 1 min less each side. For well done, cook 1 min more.
  8. 9. Take the burgers off the barbecue. Leave to rest on a plate so that all the juices can settle inside.
  9. 10. Slice four burger buns in half. Place, cut-side down, on the barbecue rack and toast for 1 min until they are lightly charred. Place a burger inside each bun, then top with your choice of accompaniment.

pork and chicken skewers

Ingredients

  • 200g boneless chicken breast
  • 200g pork fillet
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • small bunch coriander, chopped (optional)
  • cooked rice or pitta, to serve

Method

  1. 1. Cut the pork and chicken into cubes. Heat the pan over a low heat and add the fish sauce and cool. Tip in the pork and chicken and mix well so that all the cubes are covered in sauce.
Read more
  1. 2. Heat the barbecue. If you are using coals, wait until they turn white. If you are indoors, heat a griddle pan. Thread the pork and chicken onto skewers
  2. 3. Barbecue or griddle the skewers for 3-4 mins each side (you may need to cook them for longer if griddling). Sprinkle with coriander, if you like, then serve with rice or slide into pitta breads.

Pizza Margherita

Ingredients

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100ml passata
read more
  • fresh basil and 1 garlic clove
  • 125g ball mozzarella, sliced
  • grated or shaved parmesan
  • cherry tomatoes
  • basil leaves (optional)

Method

  1. 1. Put the flour into a large bowl, stir in the yeast and salt. Pour in 200ml warm water, olive oil and bring together with a wooden spoon until you have a soft dough.
Read more
  1. 2. Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste. Leave to stand at room temperature while you get on with shaping the base.
  2. 3. Roll out the dough: if youve let the dough rise, give it a quick knead, then split into two balls. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25cm across, using a rolling pin. The dough needs to be very thin as it will rise in the oven. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets.
  3. 4. Top and bake: heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 8. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf. Smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season.
  4. 5. Put one pizza, still on its baking sheet, on top of the preheated sheet or tray. Bake for 8-10 mins until crisp. Serve with a little more olive oil, and basil leaves if using.

Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients

  • 50g plain flour
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 3 chicken breasts (about 600g)
  • 1-2 eggs, beaten
  • 120g breadcrumbs

Method

  1. 1. Combine the flour, garlic powder, paprika, 1 tsp salt and black pepper on a plate. Put the beaten egg onto another plate, breadcrumbs onto a third.
Read more
  1. 2.Toss the chicken pieces in the flour mix, then the egg, and finally the breadcrumbs. Set aside on a tray. Heat the air-fryer to 200C, then cook the chicken nuggets in a single layer (you may need to do this in batches) for 10-12 mins until cooked through and golden brown. Serve with ketchup, if you like.
